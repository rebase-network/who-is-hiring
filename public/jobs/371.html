公司介绍

**mappingfunk.xyz** builds a decentralized marketplace for blockspace asset. Through the tokenization of the Blockspace, the blockspace producer and blockspace consumer can leverage onchain liquidity infrastructure to enable real-time price discovery, trading, and hedging. **Why Blockspace?** Considering the Infra layer and DApp layer in Web3 have their own native Andy&Bill’s Law, so layer1s and alt-layers just can’t eliminate the computing cost of blockspace once and for all, which means blockspace will always remain as a scarce, volatile commodity (See what happened to Intel & MS, AWS & TikTok these decades). Thus making blockchain into a truly sustainable infra towards mass adoption, i.e. how to price, trade, and hedge blockspace, the ever-scarce on-chain computing resources, at any given time, emerges as the crown jewel of Web3 industry. **Challenges** More specifically, given that Bitcoin and Ethereum constitutes the majority of the market, the lack of a blockspace-native marketplace poses numerous amounts of challenges: **Solution** - In L1/L2 industry, as over $10 billion per year of Gas cannot be managed in a predictable manner, this burden is directly shifted to end-users, preventing DApps from achieving mass adoption; - In Bitcoin ecosystem, the lack of a liquid market for hashpower has led to mining company bankruptcies and over $10 billion in annual cash flow not being captured by DeFi universe.

We are delivering MappingFunk solution by combining three building blocks

the Spot Marketplace (Over-Collateralization + Liquidation + Oracle + Uniswap) the Derivatives Marketplace (Oracle + SynFutures) Structured Products (Arbitrage Strategy Vaults + Account Abstraction Infra) **Usecases** With MappingFunk, DApp Builders (In L1/L2 industry) can spend ~2.7ETH upfront to buy a service that pays 1 Tx of Gas fee per day, or ~270 Tx of Gas fee on the 90th day for DApp users, enabling a zero-gas user experience; while Mining Operators (In Bitcoin industry) can cede 5% APY to instantly swap hashpower exposure for crypto assets, or hedge to lock in an over 3x premium compared to legacy market.
