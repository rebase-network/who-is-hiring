About

EthSign Team EthSign team is a highly collaborative cross-border team that aims to bring design concepts to real life. Our products are built upon Web3 infrastructure that has just become mature over the past few years. We aim to build a bridge between DAOs and real-world entities. We are a successful startup backed by VCs like Sequoia India , Mirana Ventures (venture partner of BitDAO ) to continue building EthSign Signatures and more tangible scenarios around DAOs. Our main product EthSign Signatures is the latest version of our e-signing product that is completely revamped to optimize for user experience and functionality. EthSign Signatures provides the same functionality, UX, and legal validity as Web 2.0 e-signing platform, while leveraging the power of blockchains to enhance transparency and security. Users can upload documents, create signing fields, customize annotation objects like text fields & checkboxes, invite co-signers, track signing status, download documents, and view events of the entire agreement lifecycle. We are also actively building a subproduct called Tokentable, a platform that enables Web3 projects to model and manage the distribution of their tokens. Built with founders, investors, and employees in mind, Tokentable is optimized to streamline the management of token equity for all stakeholders.
