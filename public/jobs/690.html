To learn more, visit http://www.synfutures.com/ **Roles & Responsibilities** SynFutures is looking for passionate and experienced professionals to join our team. We are currently hiring for two key positions: AI x Blockchain Engineer and DeFi Trader. **【AI x Blockchain Engineer】** We are seeking an AI x Blockchain Engineer with a strong background in AI, trading, and blockchain technology. This role focuses on AI-driven data signal processing, trading strategy development, and building an AI-Web3 infrastructure for DeFi. The ideal candidate should have deep expertise in AI models, algorithmic trading, and blockchain infrastructure. **Responsibilities:** • Develop AI & Web3 Infrastructure o Build and maintain a scalable AI-powered Web3 framework for DeFi applications. o Ensure robust security and efficiency in AI-integrated blockchain solutions. • AI-Powered Data Processing & Trading o Design AI algorithms for real-time data signal analysis and decision-making. o Develop algorithmic trading strategies leveraging blockchain data. • Research & Development o Stay updated on AI, blockchain, and DeFi advancements. o Conduct research to enhance trading strategies and infrastructure. • Collaboration & Innovation o Work closely with cross-functional teams to drive innovation. o Contribute to the company’s overall strategy and vision. **Requirements:** • PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics, Machine Learning, or a related field. • Strong passion for blockchain, smart contracts, and DeFi protocols. • Proficiency in programming languages for AI and trading systems. • Experience in building trading systems and financial market models. • Knowledge of trading and real-time data processing is a plus. **【DeFi Trader】** As a DeFi Trader, you will manage and optimize algorithmic trading strategies in decentralized finance (DeFi), with a focus on market making and trading on on-chain DEXs. The ideal candidate should have strong expertise in crypto markets, algorithmic trading, and DeFi protocols. **Responsibilities:** • Develop & Execute Trading Strategies o Design and optimize algorithmic trading & liquidity provision strategies. o Monitor market conditions to identify opportunities and mitigate risks. • Collaborate & Innovate o Work with smart contract developers to ensure seamless strategy integration. o Stay ahead of emerging trends in DeFi and blockchain technology. **Requirements:** • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, or related fields. • 2+ years of experience in onchain crypto trading or market • Strong coding skills in JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, or similar languages. • Deep understanding of DeFi protocols and blockchain ecosystems. • Solid grasp of financial concepts and risk management. • Ability to work independently and in a distributed team environment. **Contract details** **How to apply** Submit your CV to **hiring@synfutures.com**with title **DeFi Trader_[your name]from Rebase**, or **AI x Blockchain Engineer _[your name]from Rebase**. - Location: Based in Hong Kong (remote work options available). - Travel requirement: low. - Compensation: includes competitive base cash, bonuses, and token allocations.
