项目叙述

SynFutures ($F) is a leading decentralized exchange and full-stack financial infrastructure provider. Leveraging its innovative Oyster AMM model and fully onchain order-matching engine, SynFutures empowers anyone to list and trade any asset with leverage. With the debut of its AI assistant, Synthia, SynFutures is enhancing decentralized finance infrastructure with advanced intelligence and a seamless user experience. Backed by top-tier institutions including Pantera, Polychain, Dragonfly, Standard Crypto, Framework, and SIG, SynFutures dominates the decentralized perpetual futures market on Base with over 50% market share and ranks among the top five globally. We are seeking skilled AI Agent Engineers to design and develop cutting-edge AI agents that enhance user interactions within our DeFi ecosystem. You’ll harness LLMs, and frameworks like AutoGen and CrewAI to build autonomous agents that interpret user intent, analyze market trends, and execute on-chain strategies. While basic blockchain knowledge is helpful, your primary focus will be on creating robust AI systems that integrate with our DeFi platform, collaborating with our blockchain engineers to bring DeFAI to life. **【Responsibilities】** 1. Develop AI Agents: Design and implement autonomous AI agents using LLMs and machine learning to perform on-chain actions (e.g., token transfers, swaps, yield optimization) based on user inputs and market conditions. 2. LLM Integration: Leverage LLMs (e.g., Grok, GPT-based models) with tools like AutoGen to enable natural language processing, allowing agents to handle on-chain action and multi-step dialogues. 3. Agent Collaboration: Use frameworks like CrewAI to create multi-agent systems that coordinate tasks (e.g., one agent analyzes trends while another executes swaps). 4. Data Pipeline Development: Construct pipelines to preprocess and feed AI agents with real-time data, including on-chain metrics (via APIs like The Graph) and off-chain sources (e.g., price feeds, KOL tweets, medium news). 5. Agent Optimization: Enhance agent capabilities with techniques like RAG, LLM fine-tuning, or memory persistence for contextual awareness. 6. Collaboration: Work with blockchain engineers to integrate AI agents with DeFi protocols (e.g., Perp, Spot, Loan) and smart contracts, ensuring seamless on-chain execution. 7. Research and Innovation: Explore cutting-edge AI tools (e.g., AutoGen, CrewAI) and DeFAI trends to advance agent functionality.
