About

SynFutures is a decentralized perpetual futures protocol and supported by industry leaders Pantera, SIG, Dragonfly, Standard Crypto, Framework, Hashkey, and others, SynFutures is making strides in democratizing the derivatives market. It enables open and transparent trading on a diverse range of assets with quick listing capabilities. SynFutures has secured $38 million in funding to date. Building on its previous achievements, including a cumulative trading volume of $320 billion, SynFutures is setting a new standard for the permissionless open finance for all.

The Role

We are seeking a passionate, results-driven, and highly self-motivated Product Manager to lead our Real-World Assets (RWA) initiatives. You will be responsible for designing and delivering innovative products that make investing in premium real-world assets more accessible and efficient for users worldwide. We’re not looking for someone who just executes orders—we want a visionary Product Builder who thrives on curiosity, deeply understands user needs, rapidly tests ideas, and drives products from concept to scale. ## What You’ll Be Doing - **Product Strategy & Design:** Lead the end-to-end planning and design of our RWA product line. Develop detailed PRDs, user flows, and prototypes focused on minimizing cognitive load and streamlining the user experience. - **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Act as the key driver within cross-functional teams—including Engineering, Design, Marketing, Operations, and Compliance—to deliver high-quality products on time. Own business outcomes such as user growth, AUM, and user satisfaction. - **Data-Informed Execution:** Build robust product analytics systems to monitor performance, analyze user behavior, run A/B tests, and iterate quickly to improve key metrics and foster sustainable growth. - **Market Innovation:** Stay ahead of global trends and emerging innovations across RWA and DeFi. Turn insights into actionable product opportunities that align with our vision.

Who You Are

- Proven Product Builder: You have 2-4 years of experience owning consumer-facing (ToC) internet products, and you've been deeply involved in the full product lifecycle—from initial concept and design to successful launch and iteration. - Fintech Native: You have a background in consumer fintech products (e.g., wealth management apps, trading platforms, retail payment solutions). You possess a deep understanding of what drives user behavior and decision-making in financial applications. - A Data-Informed Owner: You have a strong analytical mindset. You don't just report data; you use it to identify opportunities, drive decisions, and measure success. You are proficient with product design and prototyping tools (Figma, Axure, Xmind).

Compensation and Benefits

Benefits Overview: Work Arrangement - Competitive base salary - Performance-driven bonus - Token allocation incentives - Full remote flexibility - English and mandarin as daily working language - 8-hour team overlap with UTC+8 required daily
