  • Design and build developer tools/libs in the Nervos CKB ecosystem.

  • Good teamwork and communication skills.
  • Preferred Qualifications
  • Existing open source library maintainer is preferred.

  • Research, write, and maintain high-quality online documentation for Cryptape developer tools.
  • Become an expert on our products from both a technical and business standpoint.

  • Survey existing layer 2 designs, including sidechains, channel networks, and rollups, and evaluate their feasibility.
  • Deliver designs corresponding to each technical approach that can be implemented on Nervos CKB, with working prototypes of their key components.
  • Write documents describing the designs.
  • Key Qualifications
  • Research experience in Cryptography/Information Security/Applied Mathematics or related fields.
  • Familiar with blockchain.
  • English as a working language.
  • Familiar with Nervos.
  • Engineering experience.
  • Published papers at CORE B+ conferences.
