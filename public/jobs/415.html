项目叙述

## About RSS3 is a decentralized network indexing and structuring open information, making it accessible and valuable for the next Twitter, Google, and OpenAI. We advocate for the power of freedom of information and emphasize its openness, accessibility and universal availability. Unlike major platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok that control vast amounts of public information, we urge individuals to redirect the power of data giants toward the public good by supporting decentralized channels and networks, and envision a world where open information empowers everyone. RSS3 was launched by Natural Selection Labs and is backed by top investors including Arweave, Coinbase, Coinshares, Dapper Labs, Dragonfly, Fabric Ventures and Hashkey. We are a globally distributed organization: all of our members come from different countries, including US, Canada, UK, China, Singapore, New Zealand, and Australia.
