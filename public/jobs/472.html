  • Digital Marketing Knowledge: Understanding of digital marketing principles and SEO best practices.
  • Independent Worker: Ability to work independently and thrive in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • Blockchain Knowledge: Deep understanding of Bitcoin and blockchain technology.
  • Attention to Detail: Strong attention to detail and commitment to producing high-quality content.
