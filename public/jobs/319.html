职责描述

  • The Smart Contract Engineer is in charge of designing, implementing and testing of the smart contract modules that act as the interfaces for Exocore network to communicate with other L1 blockchains. These smart contracts hold the restaked assets from L1 blockchains and are under control of the Exocore decentralized network. The security and gas-efficiency of these smart contracts will be the most important factors for the successful implementation of Exocore’s omnichain system.
  • You will work with a globally distributed team of top-tier engineers and collaborate with top researchers in cryptography and blockchain protocols, to implement the best smart contract system in a secure and efficient way.
  • Design, implement, and test Solidity smart contracts that can hold significant amount of funds.
  • Collaborate with a global engineering team on the design and development of smart contract modules integrated with various L1 blockchains.
  • Study and fix security vulnerabilities of mission-critical smart contracts. Develop strategy to handle security incidents in order to minimize fund loss.
  • Write comprehensive test suites on the smart contracts to ensure their correct functionality.
  • Develop web applications that interact with the smart contracts, display informational data and allow users to send transactions.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and related majors.
  • 1+ years experience on smart contract development with Solidity, Move or Rust.
  • Developed production-level smart contracts that’s upgradable, secure, cost-effective and able to hold significant amount of funds (TVL).
  • Passionate about blockchain technology and demonstrated ability to learn new technology quickly.
  • Ability to write clean, readable and efficient code with sufficient comments.
  • Critical thinking and ability to understand the inner working of complex systems.
  • Good communication skills on conveying complicated ideas and writing technical documentations.
  • Willingness to adapt to new technology or area of focus quickly.
  • Deep knowledge on Ethereum Virtual Machines and comparable VMs of other major blockchains.
  • Full-stack development experience on production-level smart contract applications.

任职要求

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and related majors; 1+ years experience on smart contract development with Solidity, Move or Rust; experience building production-level upgradable/secure/cost-effective smart contracts with significant TVL; good coding, critical thinking, communication, and fast learning/adaptation abilities; preferred: deep EVM knowledge, full-stack smart contract app experience, professional smart contract auditing experience.

项目叙述

Founded in 2023, Exogenous, Inc. is a software technology company building Exocore, an omnichain pooled security protocol powering an open market for decentralized trust. Exocore is a blockchain infrastructure that connects to other L1 blockchains to allow restaking of existing staked L1 tokens or LSDs (liquid staking derivatives) to supply token-economic security to off-chain middleware protocols or applications. Restaking is a new category in the industry with huge potential to reshape how security is modeled and utilized by various security-focused applications. ### Core Team Warren Paul Anderson, Co-founder and CEO [Warren](https://www.linkedin.com/in/warpaul1/) has been working full time in crypto and web3 for ten years.
