职位概述

## About We are looking for a visionary and technically skilled AI Engineer to take the lead in designing and deploying autonomous AI agents for real-world robotics applications. You will play a pivotal role in developing intelligent agents that can perceive, reason, and act in dynamic physical environments, coordinating across sensors, actuators, and high-level decision-making systems.

职责描述

  • Design and implement modular AI agent architectures for robotics systems, integrating vision, navigation, task planning, and memory.
  • Lead development of agents that interact with robotics middleware and hardware systems in real-world environments.
  • Integrate large language models (LLMs) for reasoning, decision-making, and tool usage in robotic workflows.
  • Build multi-agent systems with clear routing logic, role assignment, and stateful memory management.
  • Oversee deployment pipelines including containerization, orchestration (Docker), and system monitoring.
  • Collaborate with robotics engineers, ML researchers, and product teams to ensure tight coupling between hardware capabilities and agent intelligence.
  • Stay current with the latest advancements in AI agents, robotics, and embodied learning systems.

任职要求

  • 2+ years of experience in machine learning, robotics, or intelligent systems
  • Strong proficiency in Python and experience working with LLM orchestration frameworks (e.g., LangChain, AutoGen, CrewAI, Agno).
  • Deep understanding of agent-based reasoning, memory, task decomposition, and tool integration.
  • Experience deploying real-time AI applications with containerized infrastructure (e.g., Flask, Docker).
  • Strong communication skills and experience leading cross-functional technical projects.
